Heshe Treasure Map

The webmap of Heshe is created to help tourists navigate through nearby sites and connecting townships. The webmap presents information about the road map, transportation system and points of interests on the main sites. Background information and photos are indicated for each location. Moreover, suggested itineraries varying from one day trips to three days trips are also made available. This is a project is made possible by the Taiwan Nature Treasure Map Program 2016 of National Taiwan University and Heshe Nature Education Center, supported by the Ministry of Education of Taiwan.
